About Us

The Owl Line Online Bookstore established in 2017,is the online version of the Kennesaw State on-campus bookstore.
Our main purpose is to supply students who do not have an easy way to buy/rent books, with a convient way to not only
purchase/rent the recommended or required textbooks per class, but to also have those books shipped to them
anywhere within the United States.
